    3D Part Modeling Services-
    We provide 3D part modeling solutions for different industry verticals, including Automobile, Manufacturing, Consumer Goods, General Engineering Production, and Aerospace amongst others. Our team has the capability to undertake rapid prototyping of the machine parts using software such as 3D Max, Autodesk, AutoCAD, and SolidWorks.
    Our 3D part modelers and tool setters are well versed with the industry standards, and pay great attention to intricate details such as heat and stress treatments, material tolerance, welding details etc. while creating a 3D model. We are well-equipped to provide blow-up or explosive views of any mechanical parts as per your exact requirements, helping you go to market faster. Our services include -

    • Multi-view representation of machine parts (balloons and leader lines) in 3D
    • 3D machine parts modeling for automotive components and other industrial fixtures
    • 3D modeling for assemblies and collection of parts
    • 3D modeling for components in Aerospace, Automobile, Electronics, etc.
    • 3D modeling for sheet metal and plastic molding
    • 3D modeling from 2D details
    • 3D CAD models and sectional views
