Privacy Policy

Musgrawit has created this Privacy Policy to demonstrate our firm commitment to protecting information you submit.

Collection of information:

You can visit Musgrawit websites without revealing your identity. There are times, however, when we may request information to better serve you as an existing or prospective client. We collect some personal information when you register for an event, or subscribe to our newsletter, or sign-up for a form which could include such as name, job title, address, phone number and email address. These requests are made to improve communications, make content from our website accessible in a legal manner, and to enable us to provide a subscription service. It is our intent to protect the privacy of any shared information that is personally identifiable.

Use of Information:

Musgrawit does not sell, distribute, or give your personal information to any third party without your knowledge and consent except to provide products or services in which you have expressly displayed an interest, when you have given Musgrawit your permission, or under the following circumstances but is not limited to: When working with contracted outsourcing partners who work on behalf of Musgrawit. These partners are subject to strict confidentiality agreements. Additionally, these companies are not extended any independent right to share this information with their partners.
