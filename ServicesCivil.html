    BIM Modelling Services:

    BIM is as an intelligent model-based process for planning, designing and managing buildings and infrastructure projects. With the increasing popularity of BIM, producing a fully coordinated BIM 3D service model is essential for any construction project. BIM models are intelligent objects backed by geometry and data. These models enable construction team to optimize, validate as well as coordinate information regarding design within a virtual environment, making them a very significant part of the entire BIM process.
